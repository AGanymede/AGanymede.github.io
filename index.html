PROJECTS

Santa Search

To the right is a screenshot of a game I recently developed in my studio, Hard Light Intiative, for a 4 week class project in the Twine engine. In this project, I was one of the two main programmers, coding most of the variable and conditionals for the game. While I had not previously used Twine, I was able to quickly pick up the macro language predefined by our version of Twine (Sugarcube 2.0) and create the coded backbone of Santa Search. Furthermore, I was in charge of polishing of the game before release, catching and squashing mutliple bugs in the process.

Santa Search is available to play on itch.io
